About Me

Beatriz Delos Santos

Hi, my name is Beatriz, and I'm a business administration major with a specialization in marketing, from the University of California, Riverside. I came across data analytics during my last year in my university and decided to teach myself the coding (SQL and R), processing, analyzing, and visualizing (Tableau) that comes with data analytics.

From a young age, I've relished the art of problem-solving, whether it be through drawing, video editing, graphic design, brain teasers, and even through academic assignments. To me, the datasets resemble an enormous puzzle awaiting to be uncovered. My academic and hands-on experience with data analytics has shown me the real thrill that lies in discovering hidden patterns and trends, and that's the challenge I eagerly embrace, especially when these datasets are have the opportunity to make an impact in the real world.
