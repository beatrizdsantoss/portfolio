Sephora Product and Reviews Analysis

November 2023
Data Source

Background:

Sephora is a retail establishment specializing in cosmetics distribution and is committed to promote beauty. Sephora puts an unwavering focus on exceeding customer expectations by offering carefully curated beauty products. This project aims to enhance our understanding of product supply and how customers interact with products.

Goal:

Develop a deeper insight into the relationship between customers and products for an optimized marketing plan.

Analytics Business Task:

Inspect Sephora's online store data by taking a look into their products, prices, brands, variations, and reviews.




















Limitations:

  • Regarding the dataset, including the quantity of products sold and profits may give better insights on how influencial love counts and recommendations are in shaping customer decisions.
  • The dataset could also include the annual count of love counts (saves), allowing us to assess how frequently customers save products.

Results/Suggestions:

The impact of social media on product popularity is striking. Take Laneige's sleeping mask, for instance; it captured the spotlight across platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube in 2022. Even celebrities like Kate Moss, who got wind of the product through her daughter - probably from her social media feed - showered it with attention (Data Source). This phenomenon could explain why Laneige's Lip Sleeping Mask claimed the title for the most recommended, most saved (love counts), and most advocated skincare product based on the analysis above.

One of the best methods to marketing is through word of mouth. Ensure that one customer is satisfied with their product, and allow them to share their experience with the product to their friends, family, and social media followers. Word-of-mouth can be chalked up to luck; however, there are effective methods to proactively encourage it. It can be as simple as excellent customer service; referrals, where customers are rewarded for sharing with others they know with discounts or free products; and social media influence with celebrity endorsements. Creating a user-friendly way to receive product reviews, or even rewarding those who leave an honest review, can help further spread the products and the company name from customer to customer.
